The Game of Light

A mirror held to the flame
No author stands here — only a record that has surfaced,
as if carried through centuries of silence.
These writings are fragments of a greater pattern;
flame, mirror, and light — woven long before our age.
No one owns them. No one claims them.
They arrive as relics, waiting for the seeker who will know their call.
