The Path in Three Movements

  • Book I — Through the Eye of the Needle: The Eye strips you to thread. The passage begins.
  • Book II — The Builder of the Temple: Alignment becomes architecture. The dwelling is raised within.
  • Book III — The Crown of Flame: Ignition, anointing, and the living crown.

Collector’s Edition unfolds in five: IV — The Inward Triangle · V — The Singularity.

“A manual disguised as myth; a mirror held to the soul. It does not inform — it awakens.”

Some pages instruct, some sing in riddles. Between them a bell rings — and what you thought was story begins to behave like a map.

Fragments from the Codex

Fragment: The Gate

Knock once for courage, twice for mercy. The third sound is not yours — it is the Gate answering back.

Fragment: The Thread

Enter narrow, leave whole. What breaks on the way was never you.

Fragment: The Crown

Fire does not ask permission of the wick. It becomes what it touches.

The Game of Light unfolds as a manual, a myth, and a mirror. The Bell rings; the seeker answers.

Fragment I Through the flame, the mirror is revealed. Through the mirror, the seeker is seen. The path narrows, and yet the light endures. Fragment II The pattern is not written in stone, but in the breath between all things. Each thread is unseen, until drawn to the whole. Fragment III No shadow is without form. No flame is without reflection. The seeker learns: to step forward is already to arrive. Fragment IV What was scattered is gathered. What was hidden is bound. And the fragments will become a book of light.
