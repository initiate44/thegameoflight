Fragment I Through the flame, the mirror is revealed. Through the mirror, the seeker is seen. The path narrows, and yet the light endures.
Fragment II The pattern is not written in stone, but in the breath between all things. Each thread is unseen, until drawn to the whole.
Fragment III No shadow is without form. No flame is without reflection. The seeker learns: to step forward is already to arrive.
Fragment IV What was scattered is gathered. What was hidden is bound. And the fragments will become a book of light.
